Webflow clipboard inspector

Maintainer tool for the prompt library’s “Copy for Webflow” payloads.

Workflow: build (or fix) a structure once in a real Webflow Designer project → select it and press Cmd/Ctrl+C in the Designer → focus the box below and press Cmd/Ctrl+V. The clipboard’s application/json slot (Webflow’s @webflow/XscpData payload) is dumped pretty-printed below — use it to replace the hand-written payloads in prompts/index.html when they drift from what the Designer actually produces.

Click here, then paste (Cmd/Ctrl+V) what you copied in the Webflow Designer
